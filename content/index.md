# Chief Scout Knowledge Base

The tactical intelligence layer behind [Chief Scout](https://chiefscout.io) — a football scouting platform built on a 42-role taxonomy, 13 archetype models, and 140 years of tactical history.

## The Taxonomy

Chief Scout classifies every player across five orthogonal systems:

| System | Question | Scale |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Personality** | Who are they? | 16 types (4 dichotomies) |
| **Archetype** | How do they play? | 13 models, scored 0-100 |
| **Status** | What's happening now? | 5 mutable tags |
| **Tactical Role** | Where do they fit? | 42 roles across 9 positions |
| **Four-Pillar** | How good are they? | Technical, Tactical, Mental, Physical |
